Business Formation Statistics by State

The Business Formation Statistics (BFS) measure business initiation activity and the cycle from initiation to realized business formation. BFS data is acquired by reviewing Employer Identification Number applications made on IRS Form SS-4, this provides an early look at business formation activity within the U.S.

Shown here are the High-Propensity Business Applications, a subset of applications that contains all applications with a high-proprensity of turning into a business with a payroll. Maps are divided into two time-intervals: all business applications and post Covid-19 lockdown measures in March 2020. Full information about the dataset is available here.
